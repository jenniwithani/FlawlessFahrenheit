Why does this matter to us?

While the freezing temperatures of office buildings may seem like a trivial matter in the grand scheme of things, the reason why offices maintain such low temperatures is something that cannot be overlooked. The fact that office temperatures are adjusted to accommodate men exemplifies the idea that gender-discriminating bias is apparent in even the most subtle elements of our lives. We can no longer tolerate living in a "man's world", where many components of our daily lives are designed for men. More women are entering the workforce, and it's time to finally welcome them in the office, one degree at a time.
